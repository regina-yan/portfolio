Appian Product Illustrations

Illustration
In my first year at Appian, I started a product illustration initiative to introduce graphics to the platform that not only added visual interest and enhanced user experience, but also promoted diversity and inclusivity - two values that are extremely important to myself and the company.

The next year, Appian underwent a rebrand that included a new style guide for illustrations -- oof, a lot of illustrations to update! I worked with the marketing team to push the direction of the new style to continue embodying the values we had in the initial illustrations, and I continued leading our platform illustrations team to rebrand and improve upon our existing graphics.
