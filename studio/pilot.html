Springtime

Fiber Arts
"Pilot" was my first weaving project. I had just moved into a new home during the pandemic and needed both wall decor and a quarantine hobby. I watched a video by withwendy, a huge inspiration of mine, where she showed different techniques for large-scale loom weaving. I was immediately drawn to the idea of big, abstract, fiber art projects since most of the work I do is quite the opposite — small, vector-based, digital work. This project is called "Pilot" because I really had no idea what I was doing — I literally put nails into a yardstick and taped that to a doorframe to make the loom — but, this project established a tone and style for my future weavings
